CallMeNot - Delete Your Data

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

CallMeNot by AppsStudio HQ gives you full control over your data. You can delete your data at any time using the methods below.

Delete Whitelist Entries

Step 1: Open the CallMeNot app

Step 2: Go to the Whitelist tab

Step 3: Tap the delete icon next to any contact you want to remove

Step 4: Confirm the deletion when prompted

Delete Cloud Data

If you have enabled cloud sync, your whitelist is stored in Firebase. To delete your cloud data:

Step 1: Open the CallMeNot app

Step 2: Go to Settings

Step 3: Tap Sign Out to disconnect your account

Step 4: Your cloud-synced data will no longer be associated with your account

Delete All Local Data

To remove all data stored by CallMeNot on your device:

Step 1: Go to your device Settings

Step 2: Navigate to Apps > CallMeNot

Step 3: Tap Clear Data or Clear Storage

This will remove all local data including your whitelist, call log history, and app settings.

Request Full Data Deletion

If you would like us to completely delete all data associated with your account from our servers, including any cloud-synced whitelist and authentication records, please contact us:

Email: privacy@appsstudiohq.com

Please include the phone number associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm deletion via email.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Whitelist entries: All saved phone numbers and contact names
  • App settings: Your call screening preferences and rule configurations
  • Call log data: Records of blocked and allowed calls within the app
  • Authentication data: Your phone number and Firebase account link
  • Subscription data: Managed by Google Play; visit Google Play Subscriptions to manage

Data Retention

Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Cloud data is permanently removed from Firebase servers within 30 days of a deletion request. Local data is removed immediately when cleared from your device.
